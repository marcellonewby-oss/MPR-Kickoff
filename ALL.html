Tournament details
How we play

Everything you need to prepare your squad for matchday in Madrid.

⚽ Match rules

  • 7 players per side on the pitch
  • Squad of up to 10 (3 subs allowed)
  • 20-minute halves — no half-time break
  • Offside rules do NOT apply
  • Fair play enforced — referee decisions are final

🏟️ Venue & logistics

  • Held in Madrid — venue confirmed per edition
  • All matches on artificial turf
  • Arrive 30 minutes before first kickoff
  • Matching kit or official jerseys required
  • Photos and highlights published each edition

📊 Group stage points

  • Win = 3 pts · Draw = 1 pt · Loss = 0 pts
  • Top 2 from each group advance to semis
  • Tiebreaker: Goal Difference → Goals For → Head-to-Head
  • 8 teams total — 2 groups of 4

⚠️ Eligibility

  • Open to all amateur and recreational players
  • Minimum age: 18 years old
  • Any gender, race, or nationality welcome
  • Entry fee required to confirm spot
  • 8 teams max — first come, first registered

Got questions?
Common questions
How many players do I need? +

Minimum 7 to play, but we strongly recommend 9 or 10. In case of a red card, injury, or tiredness, having players on the bench is essential. Don't show up short.

Where are tournaments held? +

The first three editions were all at Rupe Sahagun, Madrid. The venue is confirmed and announced when each tournament is posted. Follow us on Instagram to stay updated.

What is the entry fee? +

The entry fee varies per edition and is announced with each tournament. The last edition was €140 per team (around €15–20 per player). We'll confirm the exact amount when you register.

Can players of any nationality join? +

Absolutely — any gender, race, or nationality is welcome. The only requirement is that you're 18 or older and can play at a decent recreational level.

What if a team withdraws last minute? +

We have a strict no-refund policy. However, if you back out at least 48 hours before the tournament, you will receive a full refund. Withdrawals within 48 hours forfeit the entry fee.
